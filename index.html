Transportation Modeling

Have you carpooled with other people in urban centers like Manhattan or Chicago downtown? Such urban centers often are characterized by one-way streets and small city blocks that may entail vehicle detours while picking up or dropping off travelers in a shared-ride service. How about reducing vehicle detours by enabling short-distance walkings in planning vehicle routes? I have involved several projects that aim to solve such a problem, either assuming demands known in advance (static) or demands revealed over time (dynamic)
